Late 16th century depiction of a native conjuror intended to show English people back home the exotic nature of the natives, upon whom European colonists were wholly dependent. The English were trying to make sense of a people with whom they did not find much in common. Chiefs and Princesses are inventions of the English, used as metaphors for their own social structures.
