In their effort to eradicate Native culture, white Americans set up Indian schools. These institutions were designed to Kill The Indian to Save the Man.
Killing the Indian has been implemented by guns and education. Samson Occum was an 18th century Indian student at what would become Dartmouth college. Founder Eleazar Wheelock wanted students at his school to return to their Native groups as missionaries, envisioning then as emissaries for their own cultural eradication. This was unsuccessful.
