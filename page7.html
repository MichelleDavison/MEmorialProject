Actors depicting Natives in redface is nothing new. Here 19th Century stage actor Edwin Forrest plays Metamora; or, The Last of the Wampanoags. This was a common theme of 19th century fiction -- the disappearing Noble Indian. Euro-Americans felt comfortable with this as they had less and less contact with Native people, especially in the East.
Metamora was based on the real Native Metacomet, or King Philip, who rebelled against encroaching Puritans in the 1670s. He was killed and his body dismembered by terrified Christian settlers who used the body parts as souvenirs.
