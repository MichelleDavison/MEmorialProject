The Washington Redskins Team Name

Controversy surrounds the Washington Redskins' (and other teams with Native caricatures as mascots) team name as Native American groups ask that the team name be changed. They charge that the name is a racial slur. Supporters of the name contend that the name is a tradition that at best honors Native people, and at worst does them no harm.

Europeans since contact have taken Native identity and tried to mold it into forms understandable to them. Natives have been alternately exotic, brutal, peaceful, 'noble savages,' brave warriors, and duplicitous people.

The actual lives of Native people has been dismissed as Europeans forcibly took both their lives and their land.
